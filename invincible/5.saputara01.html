About

Saputara hill station is situated in the district of Dang. It is a part of the Sahyadri range of mountains and the highest point is at an altitude of 1081 metres from sea level. After the very first rain of the season, the whole area comes out full of greenery, frequent waterfalls and gushing streams. Saputara Adventure Camp is all about feeling the thrill of camping in these most beautiful hills...The participants will stay in tents, visit tourist places and hike up to nearby hills. The city has been developed as a Hill Station by the government and is a hot favourite place of tourists all the way coming from Gujarat, Nashik and Bombay. DAY 1 • SEP 22, 2023

Arrival at Saputara & Activities

09:00 AM | Visit Gira Waterfall & Depart for Saputara
The bus will arrive at the Gira Waterfalls followed by a 10 min halt at a hotel for refreshment. After visiting the falls, the bus will take the participants to the basecamp.
11:30 AM | Arrival at Saputara Campsite & Setup a Tent
The first thing we will do is to set up camps. The instructors will lead all the participants to their allotted tents.
01:30 PM | Some Games/Activities and Lunch Break

Depending on the weather, we will perform some activities to introduce and familiarise every trekker with each other. After such a short icebreaking session, we will head for Lunch. Food is what Invincible is most praised for, and you will enjoy it surely.

05:30 PM | Trek to Sunset Point

We will walk towards some nearby places and acclimatize our bodies to this new weather. We will go to a place, which offers the best sunset view. If the monsoon clouds allow, that will be a nice closure of the day.

08:30 PM | Dinner

Do not miss any meals during this camp, as it is vital for a physical workout like trekking and acclimatization. Have a good sleep on this night, as you would have traveled so long.

Sightseeing & Start Return Journey

Saputara is a home for multiple beautiful gardens and they happen to be in full bloom in the monsoon and post-monsoon days.

The day would start with a trek to the sunrise point and then we will explore gardens, museums, and lake areas. After spending time in the town, we will head to the campsite for lunch.

After having lunch, we will start our return journey full of memories and photos. Sing and dance in the bus as much as you can and get ready for the routine life from the next day.

7.After Canceled your Booking, you will get your refund by account in 7 to 12 working days
In case of refund, transcation charges are not refundable by banks/payment gateways.

Reschedule of the Camp/Event:

In case of any Emergency Reason situation, we are offering an option of

- Rescheduling is possible only before 5 days of the camp departure, In the last 5 days of departure rescheduling is not provided
- For this, you have to submit valid proof for the same including a Call letter from the University & College ID Proof in case of exams and Medical Certificate with all the respective reports from the Hospitals (Signed by MBBS post or higher authorities) in case of medical emergencies
- Excluding this above mentioned reasons, rescheduling option is not available
- Also participants will have to pay the cancellation charges for travel/accommodation arrangements.

- The rescheduling options are offered only to those who would be facing the emergency other friends or colleagues of that camp have to continue as per their schedule.
